Screen Canberra:

Create economic benefit for the ACT

Employ ACT based creatives, cast, crew or studio teams

Strengthen local capability and industry sustainability

Reflect inclusive, sdiverse and authentic storytelling

Demonstrate a clear pathway to audiences



Economic Impact:
- Contribution to long term studio growth
- Strength of commercial planning
- Benefit to the ACT games sector

Creative Merit:
- Originality and clarity of the concept
- Quality of the prototype, vertical slice or supporting materials

Viability: 
- Realism of the budget and schedule
- Team capability
- Market awareness and audience potential


How to Apply:
- [ ] Pitch video up to 3 minutes
- [ ] Pitch deck outlining vision, gameplay and audience
- [ ] Project plan up to four pages
- [ ] CVs for team members
- [ ] Finance plan and supporting documentation
- [ ] Production or development budget
- [ ] Rights documentation
